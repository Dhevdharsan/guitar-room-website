How the tuner works: click a string and it plays that note's exact reference pitch as a steady, continuous tone. Pluck the matching string on your real guitar and turn the tuning peg until your string sounds the same — no beating or wobble between the two. Click the key again (or pick another) to stop. Switch to Listen mode to have the app listen through your mic and tell you if you're sharp or flat instead.
